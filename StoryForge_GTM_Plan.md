# StoryForge Go-To-Market Plan
## Version 1.0 | December 2024 | Confidential

---

# 1. Objectives
- Hit 5K users and $50K MRR by Month 6; 400 paying users.
- Establish StoryForge as the “Suno for cinematic stories” with clear narrative/continuity differentiation.
- Build a creator community that supplies feedback loops and marketplace assets.

---

# 2. Target Segments & ICP
- **Primary:** Short-form story creators on TikTok/YouTube (50K-500K followers) producing serialized content weekly.
- **Secondary:** D2C brand marketers needing rapid multi-format video iterations for campaigns.
- **Tertiary:** Indie filmmakers needing proof-of-concept visuals and mood reels.

---

# 3. Positioning & Messaging
- **Positioning:** The fastest way to go from idea to a consistent cinematic series—characters, continuity, and formats baked in.
- **Pillars:** (1) Narrative coherence, not random clips; (2) Consistent characters across episodes; (3) One-click multi-format export.
- **Tagline:** “Create a whole series before lunch.”
- **Proof:** Continuity scores, sample projects, side-by-side “AI clip” vs. “StoryForge episode.”

---

# 4. Offering & Pricing (Launch)
- **Creator:** $19/mo, 50 scenes, 3 brains, basic formats.
- **Pro:** $49/mo, 200 scenes, all brains + formats, higher priority.
- **Studio:** $199/mo, unlimited, custom brains, API access, team seats (2 included).
- **Trials:** 10 free scenes; upsell prompt at scene 8; credit packs for overage.
- **Promotions:** Founding Creator (first 500) at $15 for 6 months; Student/Edu discount 30%.

---

# 5. Launch Phases & Tactics
## Alpha (M2, 100 creators)
- Hand-selected waitlist; NDAs; weekly feedback calls.
- Tactics: creator council (10 top users), Discord server, concierge onboarding.
- Output: 20 showcase projects for case studies + pitch deck assets.

## Beta (M4, 1,000 users)
- Invite larger waitlist; gated sign-up with use-case survey.
- Tactics: office hours twice weekly; in-product feedback widget; “Beta Badge” profile for early adopters.
- Content: Tutorials (60s), “build a series in an afternoon” challenge, product changelog emails.

## Public Launch (M6, 5,000+ users)
- Product Hunt + social blitz; launch video; press mini-briefings with creator economy outlets.
- Influencer seeding: 50 creators with sponsored credits to ship Day 0 stories.
- Partnerships: CapCut/Canva community collabs; AI film festivals; edu cohorts.
- Paid tests: small-budget TikTok Spark Ads featuring creator-made reels.

---

# 6. Channels & Plays
- **Owned:** Website/landing with live samples; email drips (onboarding, tips, upgrade nudges); Discord/Slack community.
- **Earned:** Thought-leadership on AI storytelling; founder AMAs; creator testimonials; PR to tech/creator press.
- **Paid:** TikTok/YouTube retargeting; sponsored creator threads; newsletter slots in creator/film/marketing lists.
- **Product-Led:** In-app prompts for format/brain trials; “story in 3 clicks” guided flow; referral credits.

---

# 7. Content & Assets
- Sample projects per segment (horror mini-series, brand origin, classroom explainer).
- 90s onboarding demo; 30s “export to TikTok/YouTube” snippets; GIFs of character locking.
- Case studies: time saved vs. agency; consistency score before/after.
- Documentation: quickstart, reset-onboarding.html link, troubleshooting for Dexie cache.

---

# 8. Lifecycle & Conversion
- **Onboarding (Day 0-2):** Guided first scene, 10 free scenes, upsell at scene 8; collect “goal” and “format” on signup to personalize.
- **Activation Nudges:** Remind to lock characters; suggest format if user is freeform; recovery emails for failed exports.
- **Retention:** Weekly “episode ideas” tailored to selected brain; “continue your series” notification; saved presets for formats.
- **Expansion:** Usage alert when 80% of credits used; bundle with marketplace credits; seat add-ons for Studio.

---

# 9. Community & Marketplace
- Early marketplace creators get boosted placement and 70/30 rev split for first 6 months.
- Monthly community challenges (genre/theme) with featured gallery.
- “StoryForge Labs” for advanced users to test style mixing; reward feedback with credits.

---

# 10. Success Metrics
- Acquisition: CAC payback <4 months; waitlist-to-activate ≥ 30%.
- Activation: first locked scene within 24h ≥ 50%; first export within 48h ≥ 40%.
- Retention: Week 4 retention ≥ 35%; projects/user/month ≥ 2.5.
- Monetization: free-to-paid 8%; ARPU by tier meets pricing table; marketplace take-rate growth.
- Engagement: avg scenes/project ≥ 6; export completion ≥ 80%; NPS ≥ +40.

---

# 11. Launch Checklist (condensed)
- Pricing/tiers live; billing + receipts tested; refund policy documented.
- SLA/uptime dashboards green; abuse/rate limits configured; code freeze T-5 days before Product Hunt/press go-live.
- Product Hunt listing staged with Day 0 targets (Week 1 signups 3,000; paying conversions 200); press briefings scheduled T-3 with embargo.
- Analytics + event taxonomy verified (including safety toggle, provider routing, cache hits).
- Support: FAQ, Zendesk/Intercom routing, incident comms template.
- Assets: website updated, demo videos, PH listing, press one-pager, launch video, sample projects ready.
